# Phase 14 — Social preview image spec

Specification for the repository's social preview image (the card shown when the
repo is shared on social platforms). This is a **text and layout spec only** — no
image is generated here.

## Dimensions

- **1280 × 640 px** (2:1). Design so all text stays readable when scaled down to
  a small thumbnail.

## Content

- **Title:** "AI Glasses Open Database"
- **Subtitle (one line):** "Open, cited, machine-readable data on AI glasses"
- **Credit (small):** "by AI-Eyewear"

## Visual style

- **Background:** dark, solid or subtle.
- **Contrast:** high — light text on the dark background.
- **Typography:** clean sans-serif; title largest, subtitle secondary, credit
  smallest.
- **Legibility:** verify readability at thumbnail size before finalizing.

## Layout

- Title centered or upper-left, dominant.
- Subtitle directly below the title, clearly smaller.
- Credit ("by AI-Eyewear") small, in a lower corner.
- Generous margins; avoid crowding the edges (safe padding all around).

## Must NOT include

- No "#1", "best", "largest", "definitive", or any superlative/ranking claim.
- No manufacturer logos or product brand marks.
